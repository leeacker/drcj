Dr. Cindy James, PhD Clinical Psychologist

About Cindy

Dr. James started her clinical career as a registered nurse in 1985 on an adolescent unit at UCLA Nueropsychiatric Institute, facilitating groups and supervising meals with eating disorder patients. After receiving her PhD she spent a year a Post Doctoral Fellowship in adolescent psychiatry at UCLA. Later as a psychologist she became an assistant clinical professor co-directing the outpatient eating disorder at UCLA and supervising other clinicians.

Since moving to the east coast she has been in consultation with Dr Lawrence Epstein for the past 17 years, learning the modern analytic approach to facilitating group therapy. With traing in Dialectical Behavioral therapy, cognitive behavioural therapy, acceptance commitment therapy, and motivational interviewing she has integrated many approaches in individual and group therapy.

Cindy James has been practicing as a clinical psychologist in Westport CT for 20 years.
